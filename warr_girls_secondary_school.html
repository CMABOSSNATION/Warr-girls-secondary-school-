Our Heritage

The Pride of West Nile — Rising Again

Sts. Peter and Paul Warr Girls Senior Secondary School is a government-aided, Catholic-founded girls' boarding secondary school located in Warr Sub-county, Kango, Okoro County, Zombo District — in Uganda's West Nile sub-region. Once the pride of West Nile, where every girl's wish was to study here, the school is now on a powerful path of revival.

"Warr Girls gives the best opportunity for the girl child education given its affordable fees structure, human resource and available facilities." — Rev. Fr. Alfred Agwokotho

Under the renewed leadership of Rev. Fr. Alfred Agwokotho, who was posted in 2016, enrollment has grown from just 86 students to over 200 — and the school continues to grow, supported by community leaders, civil society, and the Old Girls Association.

Our Purpose

Mission, Vision & Commitment to Girls

🎯

Our Mission

To provide quality, affordable, and holistic secondary education to girls from all walks of life in Zombo District and beyond — building confidence, character, and academic excellence.

🌟

Our Vision

To be the leading girls' secondary school in West Nile — producing academically excellent, morally upright, and confident women leaders who transform their communities.

♀

Girl Child Focus

We are committed to breaking barriers for girls in the West Nile region. Every girl deserves quality education — regardless of her family's economic background. Scholarships and bursaries are available.
